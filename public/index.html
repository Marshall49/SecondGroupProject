About JustRun-It!

JustRun-It! Is a Web Application that allows individuals who love to run, be able to log their status over a period of time and will be able to see fitness suggestions throughout the course of their use of the app so that they can get the best results

Technologies Used: HTML | CSS | Javascript/JQuery | Node.JS | Express | MySQL |
